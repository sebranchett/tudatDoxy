Detailed Description

Object to compute polar motion variables x_{p} and y_{p}, describing the motion of the Earth pole in ITRS. This class combines the measured daily data as published by the IERS with the short-period variation models due to libration and ocean tides as described in Sections 5.5.1 and 8.2 of IERS 2010 Conventions.
