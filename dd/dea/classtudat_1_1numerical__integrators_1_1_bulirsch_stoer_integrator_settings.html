Constructor for variable step RK integrator settings.

Parameters
initialTimeStart time (independent variable) of numerical integration.
initialTimeStepInitial time (independent variable) step used in numerical integration. Adapted during integration.
extrapolationSequenceType of sequence that is to be used for Bulirsch-Stoer integrator.
maximumNumberOfStepsNumber of entries in the sequence, e.g. number of integrations used for a single extrapolation.
minimumStepSizeMinimum step size for integration. Integration stops (exception thrown) if time step comes below this value.
maximumStepSizeMaximum step size for integration.
relativeErrorToleranceRelative error tolerance for step size control.
absoluteErrorToleranceAbsolute error tolerance for step size control.
saveFrequencyFrequency at which to save the numerical integrated states (in units of i.e. per n integration time steps, with n = saveFrequency).
assessPropagationTerminationConditionDuringIntegrationSubstepsWhether the propagation termination conditions should be evaluated during the intermediate sub-steps of the integrator (true) or only at the end of each integration step (false).
safetyFactorForNextStepSizeSafety factor for step size control.
maximumFactorIncreaseForNextStepSizeMaximum increase factor in time step in subsequent iterations.
minimumFactorDecreaseForNextStepSizeMinimum decrease factor in time step in subsequent iterations.
